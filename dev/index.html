From there, I learned more about HTML and CSS; and just enough javascript to make myself a danger... to myself. After many years of brochure style websites, I felt it was necessary to branch out and fill in the gaps of my knowledge base and create more dynamic and interactive applications. I'm currently attending the College of Western Idaho and pursuring a degree in software development.

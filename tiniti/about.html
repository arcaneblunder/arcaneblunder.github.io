Frequently Asked Questions

Important Information to Know Before Visiting

Tiniti uses 120 volt outlets (the same as the United States). Many of our shops and hotels sell and rent out power converters.

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced. It is on the responsibility of parents to ensure their children do not drink if they are under age by law in their country.

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

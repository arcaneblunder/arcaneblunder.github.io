About

Me at a Glimpse

I grew up moving all over Washington State and eventually moved in my mid-twenties. I have been living in Boise since 2007. I would describe myself as an easy-going person. My current goal is to finish my education and start a career as a software developer. I have three dogs named Kiba, Hiro, and Magnus.

Hobbies and Interests

One of my favorite hobbies is listening to music. I find it very relaxing, and it provides a way to avoid distractions while coding. When I can, I enjoy sketching (horribly) and gaming. I love TTRPG (table-top roleplaying games) a great deal. This type of game is a great way to escape the hustle and bustle of life while also improving problem-solving abilities and communication skills. TTRPGs are also why I am enamored by video game RPGs like Divinity: Original Sin II and Breath of Fire. My goal is to create a game that can inspire someone else to pursue something they love.

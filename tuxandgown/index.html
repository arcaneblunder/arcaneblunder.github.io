Tuxedos and Gowns for All Occassions

For over three decades, Tux and Gown has provided the Treasure Valley with high quality dress wear for all occassions. From weddings to proms, you can count on us to provide a wide selection of modern and stylish dresses, gowns, suits, and tuxedos.

Preparing for a wedding can be a very stressful time, especially when you need to coordinate it yourself. Allow the wedding wear professionals at Tux and Gown to assist you in choosing a style that fits what you want and fits within your budget. We also provide consultations to help a wedding planner or bridge-to-be decide on colors that match or compliment her on her big day.

For the groom, we provide similar unmatched services. Tux and Gown provide pin-point accuracy fittings and a wide assortment of tuxedos and suits and accessories. Choose from over 4000 styles to find your perfect tuxedo.

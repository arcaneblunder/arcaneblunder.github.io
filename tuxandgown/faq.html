FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

We will do our best to answer any questions you may have.

Below are a few commonly asked questions from our customers. If you can't find an answer to your location. please feel free to call us at (555) 555-5555 or contact us online.

How does renting work?

We try to make renting as simple as possible. You decide what items you need. After measurement, we adjust the items if needed. Several days before the wedding, you are able to pick up the items to ensure proper fit. We allow a week after the event for returning the rented items. After that point we assess a late fee of $25 per day the items are not returned.

If an item is damaged, what are my options?

It is best to return the item and let us repair the damage. The damage deposit usually covers most incidental damages and stains.

Can I receive my rental items before my event?

Of course! We highly advise you trying on all the items before your event to ensure a proper fit. If anything is too tight, please call us so we can make adjustments.

Do I need to dry clean my items?

We highly advise you to not dry clean any items prior to return. It is possible that dry cleaning may cause damage to the item and would leave you responsible for repairs. It is always best to let us handle the items.
